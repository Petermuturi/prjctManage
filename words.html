Our.Genesis.Story.

Matters of the heart.

.

Yes we are thoroughly professional; and intend to stay that way.
Still, we strongly feel that business is about people; and with people, relationships
are everything. We strive for lasting relationships whether it is with our esteemed
clients, partners or these special angels.

One common feature these angels share is that they are young. Maybe too young.
With that innocent hope peculiar to the very young, they play, they ask, they
wonder and even pray.

Soon though, that hope will receive cruel blows. Fatal blows at the hands of a
malicious antagonist… cancer.

Lisa Renee found these kids at The Kenyatta National Hospital.
All young, all with cancer. And Celebrate-a-Life Initiative was born.

Lavish Affairs hosts’ birthday parties and little events for these angels. LA hopes to
inject some joy, some fun and laughter, yes, even hope in a somewhat hopeless situation.
We would be delighted to have you join us. One more party, one more moment to
forget, to hope…

“It’s great to be blessed; it’s awesome to be a blessing”.

.
