Answers to some frequent questions.

.

Our FAQ answers some of those “Oh so important” questions so that you can have more information about us and peace of mind before meeting with us.


.
  1. Who is an event manager?

    2. An event manager is a hired professional who understands and has advanced knowledge of what is required for planning events and the many details that need to be handled on the big day. An event manager is responsible for designing, planning and coordinating the event.
    At Lavish Affairs, our main goal is to make everything easy for you. We help you come up or select a theme for your event, plan for it and co-ordinate it on that special day.


  2. Is Hiring an Event Manager really worth it?

    3. Absolutely! Most people assume that hiring an event manager means wasted money, we beg to differ. Lavish Affairs prides itself in providing quality service every time. By having us there by your side to manage all the details of your event will save you a lot of time, stress and in the long run, we will help you save some money.


  3. Can you name the best and most original venues? Are you familiar with the best florists, caterers, photographers, bands and deejays in my price range?

    4. We have developed two methods that can help solve this for you:

    1. We created Theme Packs that contain a specific theme for an event and it also contains a list of the most suitable vendors for that particular theme. This greatly saves on time and also allows you to select a theme and knowing how much everything will cost.

    2. We also created an Authentic Designs package in which we assist you in creating the design for your event. We also assist you in picking the right vendors. We have an extensive list of professional vendors at varying price ranges at our fingertips. This list is constantly updated and we only refer vendors who consistently deliver quality service and products.


  4. Why can’t I do all the managing myself?

    5. Of course you can, and that is part of the fun! But it can also be a hassle: checking out many sources for each supplier category, coming up with a design, making sure all the details are in place and worrying about whether you’regetting the best deal and whether everything will turn out right.
    We have the training and expertise to help make it all come together while you relax and enjoy your beautifully crafted affair. Together, we will create and assist you through the all the processes involved. We allow our clients to take as big of a role in planning for their event as they wish, while aiding with the details that will make the day unforgettable.


  5. Will you commit to my event budget and not push me in the direction of things that I simply can’t afford or don’t want?

    6. We understand that each client has a different amount they are comfortable spending on their event and we are committed to working within the financial boundaries you set. We base our referrals, ideas, and suggestions based on your budget and the desired outcome of your event.


  6. What happens during our consultation?

    7. Lavish Affairs offers all prospective clients a complimentary one hour consultation. When we meet with one another we will provide you with an overview of Lavish Affairs, who we are, how we work and what you can expect from us; we will discuss how we approach planning with our clients, our package options and pricing.
    Consultations are for your benefit and are most effective when they are highly interactive- we encourage you to ask as many questions as you’d like and give as many ideas and suggestions as you wish!
    Under no circumstances will we pressure you to book on the spot, we wish to leave a favorable impression of us and leave you feeling comfortable and confident in your decision to work with us. We truly want you to choose Lavish Affairs with full faith in our ability to design, and to craft your ideal event.


  7. How many hours are you on site on the day of the event?

    8. On the day of the event we will arrive as early as needed to set up all the items, ensure we meet the very first vendor delivering or setting up to the last one. We will also stay after the last guest leaves to ensure all clean up has been taken care of and vendor items are squared away for pick up. A couple weeks prior to your event, you will get an in-depth timeline which will outline what time we will be arriving.


  8. Many event managers only work with exclusive vendors. If I hire Lavish Affairs, does that mean that I can’t use the ideas and vendors that I already had in mind?

    9. While Lavish Affairs has a list of esteemed vendors, we have no problem working with the ones that you already have in mind.


  9. For the day of the event, will you be willing to oversee the entire event by supervising vendors, troubleshooting emergencies, and soothing nerves?

    10. Absolutely! Our job is to allow you to relax on that day. We will serve as a liaison between you and vendors, and handle all the details of making your event run smoothly. We are calm, flexible and roll with the punches.


  10. How do I become a client?

    11. You can contact us and we can set up your initial consultation!


  11. How can I hear about all your recent events and ideas?

    12. You can do this by visiting our blog and joining the family by subscribing to our weekly newsletters.
    We post all our work on Pinterest, Facebook, Instagram and YouTube. For all the updates you can check us out on Twitter, Google+ and LinkedIn. We’re also on Snapchat, so add us to your circle and you’ll get access to our projects and our day-to-day fun stuff!
